Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18 February 2026

Welcome to FlirtApp ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data and communications remain strictly confidential.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Display name, unique User ID (UID), and Bio.
  • Communication Metadata: Call logs (caller ID, receiver ID, call duration, timestamp).
  • Device Data: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens for push notifications.

2. End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) & Content Security

  • Encrypted Messages: All text and media URLs are secured using Advanced Encryption Standard (AES). We cannot read your messages.
  • Encrypted Calls: Audio and video calls are powered by Agora and secured with AES-128-GCM2 Encryption. Calls are never recorded.
  • Screenshot Protection: The app employs system-level screen capture prevention.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database (Firestore), and notifications.
  • Agora.io: To facilitate real-time, encrypted calling.
  • Cloudinary: For securely hosting media files.
  • Google AdMob: For displaying relevant advertisements.

4. Device Permissions

  • Camera & Microphone: For calls and voice messages.
  • Photo Library / Storage: To send media.
  • Notifications: To alert you of new messages/calls.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Users can delete messages for everyone. When selected, the data is permanently wiped from our databases and cloud storage. You can completely delete your account and all associated data at any time from our Data Deletion Page or within the app settings.
